Frequently asked

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. One click in your account, no questions, no retention email. We'd rather you came back when you wanted to.

Do you take advertising?

No. The publication is supported entirely by readers. The Saturday letter has never carried a sponsorship and never will.

Is there a student rate?

Yes — half the subscriber rate, with a verifiable .ac email. Write to us and we'll set it up.

Where is the print edition posted?

Anywhere we can reach. We use a small printer in Yorkshire and post worldwide. The patron rate covers the postage.
